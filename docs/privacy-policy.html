Privacy Policy

Kamarasan — operated by PT. Mindiology Boga Lestari · Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Kamarasan mobile application ("Kamarasan", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our app to browse menus, place orders, and earn loyalty rewards at our outlets.

By using Kamarasan, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the app and process your orders. We do not sell your data to anyone.

WhatWhy we collect itWhen
Phone number To verify your identity via WhatsApp login and link your account to your loyalty profile. When you sign in
Name To address you in the app and on order receipts. When you set or update your profile
Member ID and loyalty points To track rewards, vouchers, and tier status. While you have an active account
Order history To show your past orders, support reordering, and process refunds or complaints. Each time you place an order
Approximate device location To show the nearest outlet, calculate delivery distance, and display delivery availability. Only when you grant location permission and only while the app is in use
Device push token To send order status notifications (preparing, ready for pickup, completed). If you grant notification permission
Diagnostic data Crash reports and error traces, with personally identifying fields removed, to improve app stability. Automatically when an error occurs

What we do NOT collect: we do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone audio, browsing history outside the app, IDFA/advertising identifiers, or precise GPS coordinates beyond what is needed for the order you are placing.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via WhatsApp one-time password
  • To process and fulfill your orders at our outlets
  • To track your loyalty points and apply vouchers
  • To send you push notifications about your active orders
  • To debug and improve the app's reliability
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to operating Kamarasan.

3. How We Share Information

We share specific data with the following third parties only as needed to operate the app. Each is bound by their own privacy policy.

Third partyWhat is sharedWhy
ESB (Electronic Solution Business) Order details, phone number, member ID, branch selection To process orders through our point-of-sale system at the selected outlet
Xendit / DANA Order amount, phone number, transaction reference To process online payments
Sentry Crash reports and error traces (with sensitive fields removed) To diagnose and fix bugs that affect app stability
Expo / Apple / Google Push notification token To deliver order status notifications to your device

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or any party not listed above. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored on your device using your operating system's secure keystore (iOS Keychain or Android Keystore). Other app state, such as your cart and cached menu, is stored in the app's local storage on your device only.

Data sent to ESB and Xendit is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not operate our own user database — order and loyalty data live in ESB's systems.

Despite our safeguards, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the service:

  • On your device: until you log out, uninstall the app, or clear app data
  • Order history: retained by ESB according to their retention policy and applicable Indonesian tax law (typically 10 years for transaction records)
  • Diagnostic data in Sentry: 90 days, then automatically deleted

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information (e.g., your name)
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for location, notifications, or other permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

For users in Indonesia, this policy is intended to comply with Undang-Undang No. 27 Tahun 2022 tentang Pelindungan Data Pribadi (Indonesian Personal Data Protection Law).

7. Children's Privacy

Kamarasan is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted at this URL. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you in the app.
